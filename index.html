Who are we?

  • Global Manpower Solutions

    At Global Manpower Solutions, we make sure the right people find the right jobs, no matter where they are in the world.

    Global Manpower Solutions is your go-to answer bringing in workers from abroad to fill key roles in industries with the likes of HoReCa (Hotels, Restaurants, Cafés) and construction for Montenegrin companies.

    We also help Montenegrin workers find opportunities in Scandinavian countries like Finland, Sweden, Norway, and Denmark.

What do we offer?

  • We'll support you through the process of your Visa applications, both in and out of Montenegro.

  • We'll process all the paperwork for you.

  • We'll handle travel logistics to whatever destination you're getting to.

  • We'll take care of accommodation.

  • We'll help you integrate in your local community, language & culture.

  • We keep track of your feedback, so we can do better.
